Admission Is Going On

National Polytechnic Institute (NPI) invites applications for admission into 4-year Diploma in Engineering courses for the academic session 2024-2025. Approved by the Bangladesh Technical Education Board (BTEB), NPI is committed to providing industry-focused technical education.

Admission Requirements

  • Minimum SSC/Equivalent with GPA 2.00
  • SSC from any group (Science/Arts/Commerce)
  • HSC (Vocational) students can join directly in 4th Semester
  • HSC (Science) students can join directly in 3rd Semester

Necessary Documents

  • Original copy of SSC Transcript
  • 4 Copies of Recent Passport Size Photo
  • Photocopy of Parent's NID Card
