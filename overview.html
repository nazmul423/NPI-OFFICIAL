Welcome to NPI

Empowering The Future Through
Technical Excellence

National Polytechnic Institute (NPI) is a leading technical institution in Bangladesh, established in 2011. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industrial requirements by providing world-class technical education.

Under the affiliation of Bangladesh Technical Education Board (BTEB), we offer high-quality 4-year Diploma engineering courses. With advanced laboratories, modern campus facilities, and a commitment to practical learning, we have become the preferred choice for aspiring engineers in Dhaka.
