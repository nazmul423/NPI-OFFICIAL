The Journey of NPI

The journey of National Polytechnic Institute (NPI) began in 2011 with a small group of visionary educators. At that time, Bangladesh was facing a massive demand for skilled technical professionals. NPI was established to meet that demand by offering industry-standard technical education.

Our first campus was established in the heart of Dhaka with only two technologies: Computer and Electrical. Within a few years, due to our commitment to quality, we expanded our campus and introduced more engineering departments like Civil, Mechanical, and Textile.

Today, NPI stands as one of the most prestigious private polytechnic institutes in the country, affiliated with the Bangladesh Technical Education Board (BTEB) and recognized for its excellence in engineering education.

Key Milestones

2011

Official Journey Started

Started with two departments and a small campus in Dhaka.

2015

Campus Expansion

Extended campus and introduced Civil & Electronics Technologies.

2020

BTEB Excellence Award

Recognized for maintaining high standard in practical engineering education.

2024

Modernized Lab Integration

Upgraded all labs with AI and Industry 4.0 equipment.

National Polytechnic Institute (NPI) is a premier technical institution committed to producing world-class engineers. With modern labs and expert faculty, we bridge the gap between education and industry.
