Automobile Engineering

Design • Performance • Sustainability

Introduction

Automobile engineering, a specialized branch of mechanical engineering, focuses on the design, development, manufacturing, and maintenance of automobiles. This field plays a crucial role in advancing technology and improving the safety, efficiency, and sustainability of vehicles. NPI Manikganj is a leader in automobile engineering education, preparing students for this dynamic industry.

Automotive Power Systems

Study of engine power generation, transmission, and the infrastructure required for modern vehicles.

Automotive Electronics

Design of electronic circuits, sensors, and control units (ECU) for smart vehicle systems.

Control Systems

Modeling of dynamic systems to ensure stable and efficient operation of automotive machinery.

Automotive Machines

Analysis and maintenance of high-performance engines and industrial automotive tools.
