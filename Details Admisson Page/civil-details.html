Introduction

Civil Engineering is one of the oldest and most significant branches of engineering. It deals with the design, construction, and maintenance of the physical and naturally built environment. Particularly for students at NPI Manikganj, this branch offers huge career opportunities in developing roads, bridges, canals, and modern infrastructure.

Structural Engineering

Focuses on ensuring that buildings and bridges are safe and durable under various loads.

Geotechnical Engineering

Study of soil and rock behavior vital for the safe design of foundations and walls.

Transportation Engineering

Design and maintenance of roads, highways, and railways for modern transport.

Water Resources Engineering

Management of water resources including design of dams and distribution systems.
