Mechanical Engineering

Foundation of Modern Infrastructure & Robotics

Introduction

Mechanical engineering stands as one of the oldest and broadest engineering disciplines, encompassing the principles of mechanics, kinematics, thermodynamics, and electricity. From simple tools to complex robotics, the National Polytechnic Institute Manikganj (NPI) is dedicated to advancing knowledge and practical skills in this vital field.

Career Opportunities & Alumni Success

Graduates from NPI are well-equipped to pursue successful careers in industries such as Aerospace, Automotive, Energy, Manufacturing, and Robotics. Our alumni hold leadership positions globally, contributing significantly to their respective fields through problem-solving and critical thinking developed at NPI.
