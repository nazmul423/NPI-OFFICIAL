Introduction

Electrical Engineering is a cornerstone of modern technology, encompassing the study and application of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. From power generation and distribution to advanced electronics and communication systems, Electrical Engineering is integral to the functioning of our society. At the National Polytechnic Institute Manikganj, we prepare students for the massive opportunities this field offers.

Power Systems

Involves the generation, transmission, and distribution of electricity across grids.

Electronics

Design and development of circuits using semiconductors and transistors.

Control Systems

Modeling and control of dynamic systems for machinery and industrial processes.

Electrical Machines

Study and maintenance of electric motors, generators, and transformers.
